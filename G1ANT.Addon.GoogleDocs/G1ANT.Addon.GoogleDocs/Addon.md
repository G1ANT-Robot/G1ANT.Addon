| [googlesheet.switch](https://github.com/G1ANT-Robot/G1ANT.Addon/blob/master/G1ANT.Addon.GoogleDocs/Commands/GoogleSheetSwitchCommand.md) | This command switches between opened Google Sheets instances. |
